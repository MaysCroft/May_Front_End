Geo Element

Geo is the Rock Element in Genshin Impact and is one out of 7 Elements in the game.

Geo's only Elemental Reaction is the Crystallize effect, which can interact with all elements except Anemo, Dendro, and itself. Crystal shards created with Crystallize can provide shields when you pick them up! Geo is the Rock Element in Genshin Impact, with Liyue being the designated Geo Region and Morax, or Rex Lapis, as the Geo Archon.
